Education

My favorite thing about attending Kennesaw State University was helping others when they needed assistance on a programming hurdle. Countless hours were spent in a Discord call with a classmate, working through a bug they just couldn't wrap their head around.

Computing Showcase Day (C-Day) Fall 2024

C-Day is an event hosted by the College of Computing and Software Engineering on the Kennesaw State University campus semi-yearly where projects from many different computing disciplines are showcased. C-Day includes the sponsors of projects and the event, student-lead solo and team projects, and a nice place to network with other students and professionals in the field.
